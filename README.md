# 🔬 PhD Chaos Index

> A rigorously peer-reviewed diagnostic tool for measuring your current level of academic unhinged-ness.

**Live demo:** [your-username.github.io/phd-chaos-index](https://your-username.github.io/phd-chaos-index)

---

## What is this?

An 8-question interactive quiz that calculates your **PhD Chaos Score** on a scale of 0–100, with 5 result tiers ranging from *Suspiciously Functional* to *Beyond Classification*.

Built for anyone who has ever:
- Alt-tabbed away from their actual work when their PI walked in
- Described their thesis as a "situationship"
- Sent "chapped marungi" to a labmate and had them understand immediately
- Been presented on the spot with zero warning and survived purely on spite

---

## Features

- 🌡️ **Live Chaos Meter** that fills (and shakes) as you answer
- 🔀 **Shuffled answer options** so it feels fresh every time
- 📊 **Animated score ring** on the results screen
- 5 fully written result tiers with: official diagnosis, PI threat assessment, survival protocol, a roast, and an academic citation
- Mobile responsive, no dependencies, pure HTML/CSS/JS

---

## How to host on GitHub Pages

1. Fork or upload this repo to your GitHub account
2. Go to **Settings → Pages**
3. Under *Source*, select **Deploy from a branch**
4. Choose `main` branch, `/ (root)` folder
5. Hit **Save** — your site will be live in ~60 seconds at:
   `https://your-username.github.io/your-repo-name`
